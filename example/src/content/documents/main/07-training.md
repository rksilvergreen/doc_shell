<h1 id="sec-7">7. Training</h1>
<p>Personnel assigned to flight operations or crew must complete the appropriate certification track. Training is updated when procedures or vehicles change.</p>

<h2 id="sec-7-1">7.1 Flight Controller Certification</h2>
<p>Flight controllers complete classroom training on systems (power, propulsion, life support, etc.), then simulation runs covering nominal and off-nominal cases. Certification is signed off by the discipline lead and the Flight Director. Recertification is required annually and after any major procedure or vehicle change.</p>

<h2 id="sec-7-2">7.2 Crew Training</h2>
<p>Crew train on vehicle systems, emergency procedures, and mission-specific tasks (EVA, science ops, docking). They also participate in joint simulations with mission control. Crew are not certified for flight until all required runs are completed and the crew office concurs.</p>

<h2 id="sec-7-3">7.3 Training Records</h2>
<p>All certifications and recertifications are recorded in the training database. Audits ensure that only qualified personnel are assigned to active missions.</p>
